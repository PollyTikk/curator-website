SPORGASM

mother nature's house band



Sporgasm, comprising sweaty bear Jon Whitten and succubus extraordinaire Polina Tikk, embodies a spirit that is as raw as it gets. Their music is a wild blend of genres, pulsating with infectious energy and irresistible rhythms. But what truly sets Sporgasm apart is their lyrics. Every song is an audacious exploration of ecology: hymns on phenomenology, stadium anthems on mycorrhiza and punk rock wrecking balls on symbiosis. Prepare for a musical journey that is as intellectually stimulating as it is sonically thrilling.

Are you prepared to unleash an electrifying explosion of funk, punk, and irrefutable queer sex appeal upon your stage? Are you ready to ruin your patrons for other bands? You need Sporgasm! Hailing from the heart of Yorkshire and the vibrant streets of Berlin, this dynamic duo is ready to shake your venue to its very core..
