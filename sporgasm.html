SPORGASM

Mother Nature's House Band



"Listening to their music is like recieving a bachelors in biology from James Brown, the Ramones and Norah Jones."

Sporgasm is sweaty bear Jon Whitten and succubus extraordinaire Polina Tikk. Every song is an audacious exploration of ecology: hymns on phenomenology, stadium anthems on mycorrhiza and punk rock wrecking balls on symbiosis. We're doing a show in Berlin in July, you should come. Venue tbc.
